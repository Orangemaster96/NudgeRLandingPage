# NudgeR Support

For help with NudgeR, contact Pastrana.kevin.96@gmail.com

## Common Help Topics

- Notifications: Confirm notifications are enabled for NudgeR in your device settings, then use Settings > Resync Notifications inside the app.
- Contacts: If contact picking fails, confirm Contacts permission is enabled for NudgeR or enter the person manually.
- Calendar events: Confirm Calendar permission is enabled before creating events from NudgeR.
- Purchases: Use Restore Purchases on the subscription screen if you already subscribed through the same Apple ID or Google account.
- Data deletion: Use Settings > Delete All My Data to remove local NudgeR data from the device.

NudgeR stores relationship data locally on your device. If you delete the app or device data without exporting a backup, that local relationship data will not be recoverable.
